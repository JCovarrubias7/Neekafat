ABOUT US

Neekafat Studios is a YouTube channel that features a variety of young storytellers and actors practicing their art through the medium of filmmaking. It is led by the two Ochwat brothers, Andrew and Thomas, who founded the channel in August 2009, when they were entering the sixth and fourth grades, respectively. Since then, Neekafat has grown year by year into a more and more impressive collection of short films, music videos, documentaries, and even plays.
Through this website, you can see the best of these videos made throughout the years, and view their history in these choice cuts.


Andrew Roman Ochwat


The founder and creative head of Neekafat Studios, he is constantly willing to experiment and try new things with his videos, understanding that the YouTube channel is a playground for him to further his experience and knowledge of film.
He created the channel when he was just 11 years old, and as the incline in quality of the videos shows, he grows in his skills as the channel’s main director, writer, producer, and editor every year.
He views Neekafat as more than just a hobby. Rather, he sees it a passion-fueled opportunity for his future ambitions: becoming a writer and director in the big leagues.



Tom Ochwat


Co-founder and a major source of creativity for Neekafat, Thomas is the only person, besides his older brother Andrew, who has maintained an active part of the channel throughout its entire run.
He doesn’t share Andrew’s burning passion for filmmaking but often gladly (or slightly irritatedly) helps Andrew in his many pursuits.
He will occasionally helm a project on his own, and often comes up with many of the original ideas for the films; he and Andrew combine to form a formidable creative duo.
His own personal creative interests are geared toward writing and gaming, which he sees his own future in.



Rhys Thompson


Rhys became involved with Neekafat Studios in its later years, but his impact on the channel is undeniable.
He was the creative direction of Neekafat’s OST: A Silent Hill Tribute and his surreal humor combined with Andrew’s created many a comedy, including the Paradox series.
He’s been inspired to pursue his own career in film, but also expresses a more profound desire to study psychology.



Emanuel “Pablo” Reyes


Pablo, as dubbed by Andrew and Finnian, is the longest running contributor to Neekafat Studios outside of the Ochwat brothers.
Many of his videos, including Vampire Hunters and the Imagination saga have proven to be cornerstones in Neekafat’s history.
If their long friendship has proven anything, it’s that Andrew can always count on Pablo for some zany fun to come from one of their collaborations.


Finnian Larkin


Finn was instrumental in the formative years of Neekafat.
Slightly older than Andrew, he was able to form a rudimentary structure to many of their early videos, and his works with the channel in the first season (including Nightmare on Prospect St. and Slushie), while lacking compared to more recent endeavors, are strongly set from the rest of the pack.
His direction was what gave an early Neekafat Studios its shape, and his ambition continued to spark its creativity up through the fourth season.
While not a contributor of the channel any longer, he remains in contact with Andrew, and is rumored to be busy campaigning on the northern fringe, with his magical power sword at his side.
